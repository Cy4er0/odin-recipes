Home Made Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food—a hearty Italian casserole that brings people together around the dinner table. Layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey mozzarella make it a favorite for family gatherings, Sunday dinners, and special occasions. This classic lasagna recipe uses everyday ingredients but delivers deep, slow-cooked flavor with simple steps. Whether you're making it for the first time or looking to perfect your technique, this version strikes a satisfying balance between tradition and ease.

ingredients

For the meat sauce:
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lb ground beef (or a beef/pork mix)
  • 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

For the Ricotta Mixture:

  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt & pepper
  • Optional 2 tbsp chopped parsley or cooked spinach

Instructions

Step 1: Make the Meat Sauce <>
  • Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  • Add onion and garlic; sauté 5 minutes until soft and fragrant.
  • Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, sugar, basil, and oregano.
  • Season with salt and pepper, then simmer uncovered for 25–30 minutes, stirring occasionally

    • Step 2: Prepare the Ricotta Filling In a medium bowl, mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, salt, pepper, and parsley/spinach (if using) until well combined

    Step 3: Cook the Noodles If using traditional noodles, boil until al dente, then drain and lay flat on parchment or towel to prevent sticking

    Step 4: Assemble the Lasagna

    1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Add a layer of noodles.
    3. Spread a layer of ricotta mixture over noodles.
    4. Spoon more meat sauce on top, then sprinkle with mozzarella
    5. Repeat layers until ingredients are used, ending with sauce and a final topping of mozzarella and Parmesan.

    Step 5: Bake

    1. Cover with foil (tent it so cheese doesn’t stick).
    2. Bake for 25 minutes.
    3. Remove foil and bake 20 more minutes until bubbly and golden.
    4. Let rest 15–20 minutes before slicing
